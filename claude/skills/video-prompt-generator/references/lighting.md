## 光影强度/对比

| 强度 | 英文 | 效果 |
|------|------|------|
| **高对比** | High contrast | 明暗分明，戏剧性强 |
| **低对比** | Low contrast | 柔和灰度，平淡感 |
| **中度对比** | Medium contrast | 平衡明暗，自然感 |
| **过曝** | Overexposed | 亮部溢出，梦幻失真 |
| **欠曝** | Underexposed | 暗部丰富，神秘感 |
| **高调** | High key | 亮调为主，轻快明亮 |
| **低调** | Low key | 暗调为主，厚重深沉 |

## 使用建议

在提示词中组合使用光影描述，例如：

```
Neon-lit cyberpunk street at night, pink and cyan neon signs reflecting
on wet pavement, volumetric fog, bokeh car lights, cinematic lighting
```

```
Golden hour sunlight streaming through forest canopy, dappled light
and shadow, warm amber tones, ethereal atmosphere, dust motes dancing
in light rays
```

```
Backlit silhouette against a massive orange moon, rim light outlining
the character, deep purple twilight sky, mysterious and cinematic
```
